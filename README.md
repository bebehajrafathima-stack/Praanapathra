Praanapathra 🌱
Praanapathra is a premium eco-lifestyle and smart gardening platform focused on healthy living, organic food awareness, and home gardening. The website helps users learn balcony, terrace, kitchen, and indoor gardening through interactive animations, AI-powered plant guidance, plant disease detection, community sharing, and eco-friendly tools.

Organic food awareness
Gardening community
Plant marketplace
Multi-language support
Interactive animations & games
Sustainable living education
